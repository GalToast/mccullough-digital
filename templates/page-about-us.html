How projects move

  1. Kickoff

    • Translate your goals into a clear requirements brief and success metrics.
    • Map the site structure and messaging so every page has a job.
    • Collect brand assets, copy inputs, and tool access in one secure hub.

  2. Build

    • Design responsive WordPress blocks that keep the neon aesthetic consistent.
    • Wire analytics, GA4, and essential SEO so tracking works from day one.
    • Run QA passes covering speed, accessibility, and device checks.

  3. Launch

    • Deploy to production, validate tracking events, and secure backups.
    • Record quick training clips and document handoff tasks for your team.
    • Ship a “next 30 days” action plan with growth priorities and checkpoints.
