FAQ

How fast can you launch?+
Most projects go live in about 30 days from kickoff. Kickoff happens after the initial 50 percent and onboarding assets are received.
Do I have to sign up for a monthly plan?+
Foundation is a one-time build. Growth and Launchpad include monthly management for ads, reporting, and automation when you are ready.
What if I already have a website?+
We can refresh or rebuild. Quick wins include local SEO tune-up, GA4 and Tag Manager, Google Business Profile, or a focused rebuild if that is the better ROI.
What will you need from me?+
After you approve the estimate, the onboarding form collects brand assets, copy, and access (domain, hosting, Google). If you do not have copy ready, we can write it for you.
How do pricing and payments work?+
Packages list transparent ranges; final pricing comes with your scoped proposal. Payment is 50 percent to start and 50 percent at launch.
What happens after launch?+
You will get a handoff folder (readme, deliverables, analytics, launch checklist) and scheduled check-ins at 30, 90, six months, and twelve months. Growth and Launchpad add monthly reporting.
